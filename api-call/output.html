# Marathon Endurance Training Nutrition Plan

For a 178 cm, 68 kg athlete in marathon training, here's a balanced daily plan (~3,000-3,500 kcal depending on training intensity):

## **BREAKFAST** (600-700 kcal)
- 2 cups oatmeal with berries
- 2 eggs or Greek yogurt (1 cup)
- 1 banana
- 1 tbsp honey or nut butter
- Toast with jam

*Provides: Complex carbs, protein, sustained energy*

## **LUNCH** (700-800 kcal)
- 150g grilled chicken/fish or lean beef
- 1.5 cups brown rice or quinoa
- Mixed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, spinach)
- Olive oil dressing
- Side salad

*Provides: Protein for recovery, complex carbs, micronutrients*

## **DINNER** (700-800 kcal)
- 150g salmon or lean meat
- 250g sweet potato
- Roasted vegetables
- Whole grain bread
- Small side salad

*Provides: Protein, recovery nutrients, carbs for glycogen replenishment*

## **SNACKS** (500-600 kcal total)
- **Pre-run:** Banana + oatcake
- **Post-run:** Chocolate milk + granola bar
- **Afternoon:** Greek yogurt + granola + berries
- **Evening:** Handful of almonds or peanut butter toast

## **Key Principles**
- **Hydration:** 3-4 liters water daily (more on training days)
- **Timing:** Eat carbs within 30-45 min post-run
- **Ratio:** ~55% carbs, 20% protein, 25% fat
- **Adjust:** Increase calories on high-mileage days

Consider consulting a sports dietitian for personalized periodization.
